# Launch Review

Review this Streamlit app from the perspective of a field operations leader preparing for a QSR limited-time offer launch.

I created this comment because I want the app to be reviewed beyond whether it technically works. The goal is to make sure the dashboard would actually be useful for a field leader who needs to quickly understand launch readiness and decide where support is needed.

Focus on whether the dashboard is practical, clear, and useful for identifying launch readiness gaps before a product or campaign goes live.

Please evaluate the app using the following questions:

1. Is the dashboard easy for a newly promoted or non-technical field leader to understand?
2. Are the readiness categories relevant and helpful for LTO launch execution?
3. Are the Launch Support Tiers clear and actionable?
4. Do the Launch Support Tiers accurately show whether a shop is ready for launch?
5. Do the recommended next actions feel practical for field operations?
6. Are there any areas where the dashboard could better highlight launch risk?
7. What are the top three improvements that would make this tool more useful before an LTO launch?

Please provide feedback in a clear list with specific, practical recommendations.
